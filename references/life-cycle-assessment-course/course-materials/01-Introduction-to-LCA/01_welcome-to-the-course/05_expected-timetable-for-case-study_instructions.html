In this course, you will do a case study measuring the impacts of everyday items and their alternatives. It will be broken up throughout the course laid on in the attached document. Watch the videos in this lesson for more information in the first part of the case study where you will pick a case study topic: milk vs. its alternatives OR cars vs. its alternatives. There are models based on other topics for you to read, and examine to help see what an exemplary case study looks like in each of the parts. Finally, there are templates for you to use that indicate all the things that must be included.
