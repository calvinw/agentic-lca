Based on the rubric and the templates provided, give feedback to your peers' Abstracts.

Upload and submit your abstract first, and then use the rubric to evaluate and provide feedback for 2 of your peers.

Review criteria

Your submission will be graded by two of your peers using the grading rubric that you were provided in the previous course item.

The score that you receive on your assignment will be an average of the two scores that you will be given by your graders. In order to receive your final score, you will also need to complete two peer reviews of other learners’ submissions.

Once you have graded two submissions, and two of your peers have graded your submission, your score will become visible for you to review.
