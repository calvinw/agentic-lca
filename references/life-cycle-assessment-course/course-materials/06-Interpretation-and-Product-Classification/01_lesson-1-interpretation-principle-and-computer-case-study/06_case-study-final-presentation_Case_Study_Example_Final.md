- Background
### Personalizing one’s home is the most important “aspect of home decoration”. While the main medias used for covering a wall varies, wall paint and wallpaper are one of the most commonly used.
- Objectives
### In this case study, we will be composing a LCA for Wall Paint and Wallpaper. We hope to discern how much energy and resources are needed to manufacture, use and dispose these products. At the end of this study, we would like to conclude which product and process would have the least impact on the environment.

<!-- Slide 16 -->

- Strategies for product improvement
### •1. Reduce use phase consumption, e.g. reducing the thickness of wall paint ( while still in a standard thickness).
### •2. As the material extraction and production phase are always the dominant processes, thus it is essential to increase product lifetime and reuse of parts, e.g. use the recycled paper and prolong the lifetime of wall paint and wallpaper.
### •3. Dematerialize: obtain more service from a similar or reduced materials quantity, e.g. use kaolin or limestone to replace titanium dioxide.
