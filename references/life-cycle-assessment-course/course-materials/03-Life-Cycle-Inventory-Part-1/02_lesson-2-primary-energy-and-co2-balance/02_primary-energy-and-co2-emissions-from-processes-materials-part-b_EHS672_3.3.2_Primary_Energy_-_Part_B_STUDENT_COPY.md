- Summary
- The ratio between final energy and primary energy defines the energy efficiency of the supply chain.
- A part of the primary energy is nonrenewable, which means that the basic resource of this energy is non-replaceable or is replaced very slowly through natural processes
- There are large differences of several orders of magnitude between the energy and the CO2 emission per kg of different materials. These cannot be directly compared.
