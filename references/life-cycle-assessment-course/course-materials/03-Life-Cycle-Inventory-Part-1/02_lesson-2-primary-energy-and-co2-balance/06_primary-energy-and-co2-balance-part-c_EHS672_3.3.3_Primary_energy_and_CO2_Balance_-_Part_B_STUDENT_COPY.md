- Summary
- The primary energy balance and inventory of CO2 emissions is calculated by multiplying the amount of each database process of the process tree by the aggregated emission per unit of this process.
- Energy content and carbon are related for fossil fuels
- The ratio of gCO2/MJnon renewable primary energy can be used to check the consistency of inventory data
- Crucial to account for additional releases during use phase (e.g. combustion emissions) and disposal (e.g. incinerator emissions)
