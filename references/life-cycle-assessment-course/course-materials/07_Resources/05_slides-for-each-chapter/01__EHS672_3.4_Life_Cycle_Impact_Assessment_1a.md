## 1920s: Dobson begins study of stratospheric ozone

## 1985:
## British Antarctic Survey team, which had been measuring the ozone layer over the South Pole for 18 years, discovers the Antarctic ozone hole. - Life cycle impact Assessment steps
## Classification: Classify emissions and resource extractions into all relevant midpoint categories on which they have an effect.
## Midpoint characterization: Weight emissions and extractions using natural science-based factors to assess their contribution to each midpoint category impact score.
## Damage characterization: Quantify the science-based contribution of each midpoint category to one or more damage categories associated with main areas of protection.
## Optional steps:
## Normalization: Compares the contribution of the considered service to the current total effect at global, or continental level for each (midpoint or) damage category.
## Weighting: Weight and compares the damage results based on social, political, and ethical values
