- Summary
- The process tree is an efficient way to visualize the product system.
- The intermediary flows enable us to determine the amount of each process used per function unit, in the unit required by the LCI database.
- This will serve as the basis to then determine the resource extraction and emission elementary flows of the Life Cycle inventory.
